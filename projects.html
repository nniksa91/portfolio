Second Project M.E.G

As weeks went by and we learned more and more complexe thing on course i started developing my own mobile appplication . 

The idea was to make a social marketing application in which people with ( OPG , small company bussines ) can send offers to big chain stores in Croatia to buy out their products . As from then i started making a mockup of the application and after i finnished a mockup i started coding . First page I did was a login page , starting from just a looks of it I managed to make it nice and clean but the problem was make it interactive and at the end i managed to do it . 




Second page was a register page with a lot of fields which needed to be filled in with user data . Problem here was to store those data on the server so users can fetch them through Profile page and edit them when needed . Third page was a home page the one with lots of adds from users for various products they are trying to sell . After finishing a layout of that page i needed to connect it to adds page in which users can write a short description , price and stock of their product . 

As time is passing by little by little the application is geting more and more complex but it's starting to take it's final shape.
